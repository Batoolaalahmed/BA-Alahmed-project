Photoshop Projects






I love drinking Tea! So, I created a campaign for a tea company named “Pukka”. I used medieval Dutch portraits paintings as bases for the posters. The idea is to add royalty and empowering feel to the product. I altered the paintings so that the portrait is holding a cup of tea. In each of the three posters, the color of the tea box was chosen so that it fits the color scheme of the paintings.
